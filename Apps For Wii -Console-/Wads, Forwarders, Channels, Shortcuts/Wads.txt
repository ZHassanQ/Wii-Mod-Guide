The forwarders are looking for the file located on:

SD:/apps/APP_NAME/boot.dol
SD:apps/APP_NAME/loader.dol
USB1:/apps/APP_NAME/boot.dol
USB1:/apps/APP_NAME/loader.dol

And to Install them 

Copy your .wad's to your SD Card like this
SD:\WAD\WAD_NAME.wad

Launch "WiiModLite" through "Homebrew Launcher"

1- Go to WAD MANAGER
2- Press "+" button to install all
